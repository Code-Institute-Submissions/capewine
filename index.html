About Us

Our Story

Cape Wine was founded in 2010 when the world turned its eye to South Africa for the 2010 soccer Worldcup. The founders are passionate about the Cape Winelands and the gloarious selection of wines they have to offer.

Our small and dedicated team is passionate about these wines and want you to discover them and the collective of winemakers pushing the boundaries in terms of creativity and style.
